About Katten Leifde

My partner and I have always been keen on setting up our own western cafe with the creation

of homecook food and coffee & beverage speciality. Our objective is to comfort our customers

with the feeling of being at home

with our warm welcoming of food & drinks.

We are inititally inspired by Mellowers Cafe from their seasonal coffee and latte with both

creation and taste. Thus, that's when we started with our cafe layout and menu.

Our goal is once we have a stable income, we hope to collaborate with banks in Singapore

and first open a small simple cafe selling small bites and drinks. Followed by expanding

our menu and selling my own merchandise

such as comics book, cafe pins and designed cups.
